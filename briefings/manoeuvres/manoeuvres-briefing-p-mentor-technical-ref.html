⚠
Oil Pressure — Cold Start
Oil pressure may read up to 7 bar on cold start. Allow engine to warm at 1400 RPM minimum. Do not apply power until pressure drops to normal range (2.0–5.0 bar). If pressure does not drop within 30 seconds, shut down and investigate.

Performance Factors
Published figures are AFM reference values at ISA, MTOW, sea level, zero wind, paved surface. Increase T/O distance by ~10% per 300 ft pressure altitude, ~5–7% for grass. Headwind reduces distance; tailwind significantly increases it — max tailwind component per SOP.

Negative G — ROTAX Restriction
ROTAX 912 iSc 3: negative g not to exceed −0.5 g for more than 5 seconds. Prolonged negative g will cause oil starvation and engine damage.
